## Image Requirements

### Hero Images (1920x1080px or higher)
- High-quality, professional photos
- Good lighting and composition
- Represents your hatchery well
- Can have text overlay

### Section Images (1200x800px minimum)
- Clear, focused shots
- Good resolution
- Relevant to the section content

### Gallery Images (800x600px minimum)
- Various aspects of your operations
- Mix of wide shots and close-ups
- Show facilities, processes, team

## Image Optimization Tips

1. **Compress images** before uploading (use tools like TinyPNG, ImageOptim)
2. **Use JPG format** for photos (smaller file size)
3. **Use PNG format** for logos or graphics with transparency
4. **Recommended max file size:** 500KB per image
5. **Maintain aspect ratios** as specified above

## Quick Start

1. Collect all your hatchery photos
2. Rename them according to the structure above
3. Place them in the respective folders
4. Refresh your browser to see the images
